About the Project

Project Duration: 25 - 31 March 2021.

The purpose of this project is to design an attractive and simple website for a talented rapper based in Milan. The website will serve as a central hub for the artist, showcasing their music, biography, upcoming events, and other relevant information. The goal is to create a visually appealing and user-friendly website that reflects the artist's unique style and brand while maintaining a clean and straightforward design. The focus will be on providing a seamless user experience and ensuring that visitors can easily access the artist's content and stay updated on their latest news and releases.
