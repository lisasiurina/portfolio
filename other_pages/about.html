ABOUT KYSON DANA

Kyson is a San Francisco based designer with over 9 years experience. He’s worked on a variety in-house brands ranging from small non-profits to large tech companies with ambitious goals. He has a unique talent at seeing the big picture potential in a product or brand and then bringing it to life.

Each company Kyson has worked for has carried a strong mission behind the work—ranging from sustainability and transportation, to education. He tries to keep that same ethos for doing good in his personal life as well. He strongly believes that design and storytelling is a powerful medium to create change.

Kyson is comfortable working on a range of projects from designing native apps to collaborating on set with a production crew. Whether he’s behind the camera, illustrating on paper, or crafting prototypes on his laptop, Kyson always brings a quality of work and creativity that is hard to match.

Outside of work Kyson finds inspiration through backcountry skiing, surfing at dawn, and traveling with his wife and little boy. His hunger and curiosity for life drives him to experience everything and document all that it has to offer. This thirst for life is evident in all of his work. Kyson doesn’t find a strong line between creativity, work, and play.
